Our Process

At Century Tech Solution, we are aware that our clients place a high importance on project clarity and cost predictability. We provide a Our Process engagement model as a result, which is ideal for projects with precise and fixed requirements. We will collaborate closely with you to thoroughly define the project's scope, taking into account all features, functionalities, and deliverables.
After all of the details are well understood and recorded, we will decide on a set cost for the full project. This means that, regardless of how long it takes our development team to finish the project, you will know precisely how much you will be paying up front.

Suited for:

long-term initiatives that call for constant improvement and close coordination with the development team.

Size of Project:

large to medium-sized projects.

Requirement Type:

can be changing, but only if the overarching project goals are well understood.

Scalability:

offers a reasonable degree of scalability because team sizes can be changed, but careful preparation and communication are needed.

Project Management:

permits the customer and the development company to share ownership of the project through a collaborative project management method.

Time Frame:

Adaptable and dependent on the complexity and scope of the project.

Client Involvement:

Depending on the extent of cooperation and desired control, order from low to high.

Budget:

Semi-predictable, having monthly rates that are set and subject to alter in response to scope modifications.
